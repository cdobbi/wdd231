Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Quilt Creations website is to provide a platform for the owner to showcase her quilting projects, sell quilts, and offer quilting services. The site will also provide information about the owner and her quilting experience.

Audience

The primary audience for the Quilt Creations website is for individuals who are interested in having their projects bound and quilted by a professional longarm quilter. The secondary audience is for individuals who are interested in purchasing a quilt, or are looking for inspiration for their own quilting projects.

Dynamic Elements

The Quilt Creations website will include the following dynamic elements:

  • Quilt Gallery
  • Quilt Shop
  • Quilting Services
  • Contact Form

Proposed Implementation

* The Quilt Creations website will be built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
* The site will be responsive and accessible, with a focus on user experience.
* For quilt purchases, a third-party service like Etsy or Venmo will handle transactions.
* The site will be optimized for search engines to increase visibility.
* For the contact form, I will use a dynamic interactive form that will send an email to the owner when submitted.
* There will be a simple calculator to estimate the cost of quilting, binding, and other services.
* Clicking on a quilt image will open a larger version in a modal window.
* Dynamic JavaScript ot handle the image display and close button.
* For the Quilt Shop page, I'll use an array of quilt objects to store information about each item for purchase (quilt_patterns, quilt_images, item_description), dynamically create and display quilt cards on the page using JavaScript forEach or map to iterate over the quilt array.
