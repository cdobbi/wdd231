Gallery

Traditionally, multi-layered textiles were composed of three layers of fiber: a woven cloth top, a layer of batting or wadding, and a woven back combined using the technique of quilting, the process of sewing the three layers together.

Preferable Lime Green

Lime green is a color that is associated with freshness, energy, and growth. It is a color that is often used in quilting to add a pop of color and interest to a quilt.

Forever Valentine

A Valentine's Day quilt is a great way to show your love and appreciation for someone special. It is a thoughtful and heartfelt gift that will be cherished for years to come.

Easter Bunner Wall Hanger

A wall hanging is a small quilt that is designed to hang on a wall. It is a great way to add color and interest to a room, and can be changed out seasonally or whenever you want to freshen up your decor.

Log Cabin

The Log Cabin quilt block is one of the most popular and recognized of all quilt blocks. It is a basic block that can be arranged in many different ways to create many different quilt patterns.

Favorite Cherries

Quilted Cherries are one of my favorites. They remind me of large family gatherings around picknick tables, picking cherries in my grandparents orchard. the sweetness of life and the importance of cherishing the moments we have..

Heirloom

An heirloom quilt has been passed down through generations of a family. It is a quilt that has been made with love and care, and will be treasured by the family for many years.
